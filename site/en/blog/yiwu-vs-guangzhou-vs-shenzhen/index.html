Yiwu vs Guangzhou vs Shenzhen: which market to buy from?

Updated June 2026 · by OpenQuote, on the ground in Yiwu, China

Short answer

Pick the wrong market and you waste a whole trip. Small-commodity goods (jewelry/toys/stationery/gifts/home/wigs/festive) → Yiwu, low MOQ and mixed batches; clothing/shoes/fabric → Guangzhou; 3C electronics → Shenzhen. Also: hardware → Yongkang/Jinhua, northern small goods → Linyi, lighting → Zhongshan Guzhen. But for the widest small-commodity range at the lowest MOQ, it is still Yiwu.

Three steps to the right market

  1. Category first: match the table above. Almost any small-commodity mix can be sourced in one stop in Yiwu.
  2. Then MOQ: small batches, mixed styles, test runs → Yiwu is friendliest; bulk/custom (apparel/electronics) → go to the matching industrial cluster.
  3. Customization? Private-label/tooling usually means dealing with a factory (not always in Yiwu); in-stock mixed batches are fastest in Yiwu.

The most common mistake

Trying to buy everything in Yiwu. Yiwu is best for in-stock small commodities; bulk apparel, fabric and complex electronics often will not give you the best price or range there — do not force it, or you waste the trip.

Frequently asked questions

Can I buy clothing in Yiwu?+

Small items (socks, underwear, knitwear, scarves) yes, with low MOQ; but for bulk garments and fabric, Guangzhou gives better range and price.

Where should I buy electronics?+

Shenzhen (the Huaqiangbei area) is the hub for 3C electronics and digital accessories; Yiwu only has basic phone accessories.

Can I visit several markets in one trip?+

Where is cheapest for small test batches?+

Yiwu — low MOQ and mixed styles, ideal for e-commerce and test runs.
