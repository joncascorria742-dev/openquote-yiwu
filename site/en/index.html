FAQ

Let's be clear up front.

How do you charge? Do you take a commission?+

We take no commission or kickback from suppliers, and we never mark up the goods. Research (price check / discovery) is a fixed fee; if you want us to handle the whole order, we charge a transparent 5% service fee (paid by you, itemised, never hidden in the goods price).
Full handling covers everything: (1) sourcing & price-checking; (2) negotiating and bargaining on your behalf, including payment and lead-time terms; (3) placing the order and helping you pay the supplier directly; (4) samples — requesting and shipping them for approval; (5) supervising production, order follow-up and chasing delivery dates; (6) inspection / QC (against the approved sample + on-site photos/video); (7) consolidating goods from several suppliers, packing and labelling; (8) logistics — coordinating the freight forwarder, booking and arranging sea / air / courier; (9) customs — export declaration + help with your destination-country import paperwork; (10) communication and translation throughout; (11) after-sales, disputes and returns coordination. You pay the supplier and the forwarder directly; we earn only this 5% service fee.

Will you mark up the product price?+

No. The supplier's original quote is passed on unchanged; we don't touch the numbers, and any third-party cost is listed separately.

What do I get for $29?+

Real quotes for 1 product from 2 real suppliers, including MOQ and lead time, plus dated on-site Yiwu photos/video.

Do I get the supplier's contact details?+

Yes. The paid delivery pack includes the supplier contacts we gathered for you; what you do next is your call.

Do I have to come to China first?+

No. Get the market and prices clear remotely first, then decide whether a trip is worth it.
