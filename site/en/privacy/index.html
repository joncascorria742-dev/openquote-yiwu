Privacy policy

Last updated: June 2026.

Website requests

This website has no form and does not collect or store your sourcing request. The contact button opens a WhatsApp chat.

WhatsApp messages

If you message us on WhatsApp, we may receive your WhatsApp profile, phone number, photos and the information you send. We use it to review your request, prepare a fixed-fee scope, reply, and deliver any service you choose to buy. We do not sell your contact details.

Service providers

We may use hosting and messaging providers. WhatsApp processes messages under its own terms. If you request optional support, relevant information may be shared with a supplier or third-party provider only as needed.

Your choices

You can ask us to correct or delete the contact information you shared, and to stop sending non-essential messages.
