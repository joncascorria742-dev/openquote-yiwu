Why online prices aren’t the real price

Alibaba, Made-in-China, even 1688 show asking prices, not the price negotiated at the booth — usually higher, often “contact for price.” And 30–50% of “factories” on Alibaba are trading companies who buy from the factory and resell to you. So the number you see can hide a trader’s margin, an agent’s commission, and platform padding all at once.
