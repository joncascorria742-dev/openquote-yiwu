How to buy from Yiwu without traveling

Updated June 2026 · by OpenQuote, on the ground in Yiwu, China

Short answer

You don’t need to fly to China. A person in Yiwu can verify the real price at the booth, take photos/video and arrange shipping — you see it all remotely and decide. OpenQuote, from US$29: 1 product, 2 real suppliers, with on-site proof.

From US$29 · remote check

Why people fly to Yiwu

Because the real price, the real supplier and whether the goods match used to be visible only on site. But a trip costs real money: flights and hotels, a visa, the language barrier, time — and first-timers are easy to overcharge.

How remote sourcing works

Outsource the “being there” to a local person. You shortlist online and explain what you need; they go to the booth, verify the real price, take photos/video, ask about MOQ and lead time; you see the real picture and decide. No travel, no Chinese required.

What the person on the ground does

  • Verifies the real price at the booth (not the online asking price);
  • Sends photos/short video of the booth and the goods;
  • Arranges samples, inspection, consolidation and shipping when needed;
  • Passes original quotes unchanged; paid only by you, no markup.

Start with US$29

No big commitment up front. US$29 verifies 1 product with 2 real suppliers and on-site proof; if it’s solid, go further. See the market remotely before deciding whether a trip is worth it.
